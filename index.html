The Rhythm experience

More than an app — a daily companion that grows with you

1. Tell us your story

Not just age and weight. We ask about your energy patterns, stress levels, relationship with fitness, and what truly motivates you. For women: cycle length, symptoms, phase awareness. For men: sleep habits, recovery priorities, fitness history.

2. 60-second daily check-in

Slide to log your energy, mood, soreness, and sleep. For women: cycle phase and symptoms. Takes less than a minute, but the insights last a lifetime.

3. Your rhythm, your feed

Workout suggestions that match your phase or recovery score. Nutrition tips based on your cycle or goals. Mindfulness exercises when stress is high. All tailored to you in real-time.

4. Meet your fitness companion

Your personalized character evolves with you. It celebrates streaks, suggests rest when you need it, and shows up differently based on your energy. Some users describe it as "a coach who actually gets me."

5. Weekly discoveries

Every Monday, get a personal insight: "Your mood is 40% better on days you sleep 7+ hours." or "Your energy peaks 3 days after your period starts." Patterns you'd never notice on your own.

6. Grows with your life

Starting a family? Entering perimenopause? Training for something new? Recovery focus shifting? Rhythm adapts as your body and goals change. One app for every season of life.
